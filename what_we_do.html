What we do

Our management packages are the most cost effective in the industry and you wont find any hidden fees or additional charges. No fees are due upfront only upon successfully letting your property.

1

Landlords

As a landlord you can either register your own property details and sign up for your package directly via the website, or alternatively, contact us by phone and one of our new business team will talk you through your options.

what we do...

  • Send out our terms of business

  • Setup Online Account

  • Load basic property details to the site

  • Provide Initial Valuation (if required)

  • Arrange Marketing and key collection

  • Arrange legal documents and certification (if required)

2

Initial Visit

On our first visit our agent will take property particulars, photographs and carry out all the necessary legal checks.

It is important that as a landlord you are kept in the loop which is why you are notified at every stage of the process so you will receive emails updating you of when the marketing is taking place and a link to an advert you can amend at any time from your landlord dashboard.

What we do...

  • Arrange access

  • Advise you of day and time property details will be taken Create Advert

3

Property Advertising

We will inform you of when your property has gone live on the property portals. We advertise on Rightmove, Zoopla, Primelocation, OnTheMarket and all there affilates.

what we do...

  • Load Property Details to Property Portals

  • Inform you of when the property is made live

  • Update you on when viewings are taking place and number of people attending

  • Update you with regular feedback

4

Property Offer

All tenants are provided with a link to a feedback form and rent offer form prior to viewing a property which collects the necessary information prior to referencing so working with you we can make a decision as to who is the best applicant to move forward with referencing.

All offers are sent to yourself for approval and we will provide feedback from our agent also as to the most suitable applicant.

Once approved we will collect the holding rent from the tenant and set them up an online account which links them to your property and provides a visual aid on your landlord dashboard to quickly see where we are at in the process. We also email you at every stage to keep you updated.

What we do...

  • Collect Feedback and Offers

  • Send Offers to you with our recommendations

  • Approve an Offer with Applicant

  • Setup Online Account for Applicant

  • Link Applicant to your Property

  • Collect Holding Rent and inform you when it has been received

5

Pre-Tenancy

Once the holding rent is received we send out a referencing link to the applicant. We receive the credit check shortly after and update you of the outcome.

We then await the final details of the check which are employment verification, income verification and previous address verification. Once received we will advise you of the outcome.

If everything passes we will then arrange a check in date and inform you of when this has been booked. We then collect the rent and deposit, send out the tenancy agreement and advise you of when this is sent and signed.

We also change over all utilities and arrange an APIP, Propertymark or AIIC inventory clerk to conduct the check.

what we do...

  • Send you credit check results

  • Send you completed reference results

  • Advise you of check in date with Apip, Propertymark or AIIC Inventory Clerk

  • Collect rent and deposit

  • Register deposit

  • Inform you of when the agreement is sent and signed

  • Change Utilities into Tenant name and inform you of changeover

  • Load Documentation to your portal and serve it on the Tenant

6

Management Services

Throughout the tenancy will will advise you of when inspections are being booked, when you can increase the rent, when the contract is due to expire so you can chose to renew or go periodic.

All maintenance is dealt with inhouse and you are updated throughout the process from when the job is reported to its completion and we will not proceed with any works without your confirmation.

Rent is collected via standing order and paid to you by bacs within 3 to 5 working days of its due date complete with a management statement.

What we do...

  • Organise Maintenance

  • Advise you of rent increase dates

  • Advise you of inspections

  • Advise you of Contract Expiry dates

  • Advise you of certification expiry dates

  • Collect rent

  • Deal with day to day management issues

7

Legal

Dependent on the selected package we will send all legal notices as required. We can also offer additional products such as rent guarantees (only available where the tenant is referenced through ourselves) which mean that if, for whatever reason, the tenant is unable to pay the rent and starts to get into arrears an insurance backed scheme will kick in and ensure that you, as the landlord, continue to be paid.
