About us

Lettingsupermarket.com was founded in 2012 by experienced lettings experts.

With a passion for property, we provide a higher quality service and more cost effective solution for Landlords when compared to traditional High Street Agents or online alternatives.

By removing all unnecessary costs, we offer substantial cost savings without any impact on customer care or quality of service.

Our team of experts can assist you with any aspect of the lettings journey at anytime, regardless of location or size of your portfolio.

Service

We don’t just want to be the most affordable Agency, we also want to offer the best service. We are committed to keeping both Landlord and Tenant up to date and providing them with all the necessary documentation required, which is why we have made available an online storage facility for both Landlord and Tenant documents.

To learn more about our letting services please click here.

Lettingsupermarket.com are regulated by Propertymark/ARLA (Association of Residential Letting Agents) and all client money is protected by the the propertymark client money protection scheme.

We use qualified Inventory Clerks accredited by APIP (Association of Professional Inventory Providers) we carry out all the duties of a high street Agent from taking particulars, carrying out viewings, performing inventories, checking in your new Tenants and regular inspections dependent upon which package has been chosen.
